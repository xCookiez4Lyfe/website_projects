Meet the Pocket Portal

Product Features

The Pocket Portal has its own one-of-a-kind ez teleport feature where you can just enter the coordinates or name of the destination and it will teleport you to the location in a matter of two steps - similar to how you would operate a GPS.

AI Voice assistant

The built-in AI voice assistant allows for voice activated commands as well for an easy hands-free operation. Plus, our built in voice assistant is trained to only recognise your voice with the advancement of AI technology.

Solar powered battery

The awesome battery life of the Pocket Portal lasts up to a week without charging and it uses solar energy which is sustainable for the environment. And for people who always forget their chargers on vacation, you wont need to worry because you don't need one!

User safezone

Each user can designate their own safezone to prevent other people from teleporting into the area without permission. Users can set their own safe word for instant teleportation back to your home or default location. If you ever feel unsafe or get caught in a dangerous situation, the AI assistant can sense the surrounding environment to activate safe mode.

Project Development History

Product concept

March 2023

It took several years for our design team to come up with a concept of the Pocket Portal. We had to bend the laws of physics and experiment with travelling through a completely new medium.

Prototype design

May 2023

Once the design was acomplished, our development team and engineers started working tirelessly to come up with a functioning prototype of the Pocket Portal. Thanks to the framework established by our scientists, the prototype was a success!

Product launch

June 2023

Finally we come to the launch of our first ever product, the Pocket Portal! It will be available for pre-orders in July. Our products will also be shipped to tech stores near you in the coming months and it will be available for everyone to purchase.
